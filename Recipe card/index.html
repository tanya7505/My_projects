Cutting Chai

  • Ingredients:paani,dudh,chai patti,adrak,long,elachi,cheeni.
  • Steps:
    1. Boil water with chai patti.
    2. Add milk,ginger,long,elachi and sugar in it.
    3. Boil it as much as you want.
  • Note: More boiling gives more flavours.

Java Coffee

  • Ingredients:paani,dudh,coffee powder and sugar.
  • Steps:
    1. Take a cup and add some sugar and coffee in it.
    2. Add some water and stir it to make a creamy paste.
    3. Boil the milk and add the creamy paste to it.
    4. Add some more sugar if you want to.
  • Note: Java and coffee-- the best combo.

Mayo Sandwich

  • Ingredients:Bread,Mayo,Red-sauce,vegetables,Salt,Chilli Flacks.
  • Steps:
    1. Take all the chopped veges in a bowl and add mayo and some sauce in it.
    2. Add the salt and chilli Flacks and prepare the filling.
    3. Toast the bread from both sides..
    4. Fill the fillings between two breads.
  • Note:Cut the bread in triangle shape and enjoy.

2-mins Maggie

  • Ingredients:Maggie,vegetables,water,Ghee.
  • Steps:
    1. Take a fry pan and saute the chopped vegetable in ghee.
    2. Add water accordingly and all the maggie.
    3. When the water is boiling add the maggie masala.
    4. If you want the soupy maggie then remove it from pan early.
  • Note:Add some chilli Flacks and origano for more flavours.

Khichdi

  • Ingredients:Socked rice,vegetables,Spices,Salt,Water.
  • Steps:
    1. Saute the chopped vegetables with spices.
    2. Add cutted potatos and some water to boil.
    3. When the potatos get soft add the rice to it and wait to cook.
  • Note:Serve it hot with ghee for good flavours.

Hostel cake

  • Ingredients:Biscuit, milk,sugar,any chocolate, ENO
  • Steps:
    1. Crush the biscuits into powder and add milk to it to make a paste.
    2. Take some water in the kettle and boil it.
    3. Add the ENO to the paste and put the vessel in the kettle.
    4. Wait for 15 mins and then check the cake with a tooth pick.
    5. Melt the chocolate and put it on the cake and enjoy!!
  • Note:Move your hand in one direction while making the paste
